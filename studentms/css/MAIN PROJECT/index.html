Adventure

Yelagiri Located in :Tirupattur

Area of yelagiri hills : 30 KM

Height of Yelagiri hills : 1100 Meter

Climate in Winter season : 12-25 Degree Celsius

Population in Yelagiri is: 4409

It is a popular destination for nature lovers and weekend getaways.
Its rocky hills and natural beauty make it an ideal escape from city life.

PLace to Visit

NATURE PARK

Open Time : 7.00 AM - 8.00 PM
Cost : Rs.15/- for adults and Rs.10/- for children.
Place : Punganoor


The park is the best destination for all ages of people and provide a feeling of relaxation and stunning environment. It consists of diversified and enormous numbers of flora. There are number of things such as artificial cascade or the waterfall, children's park, musical fountain with multi-coloured lighting, fish aquarium, seasonal garden, bamboo house, poly house and many more to engage kids and even elders for a long time.

BOAT HOUSE

Open Time : 7.00 AM - 8.00 PM
Cost : Rs.15/- for adults and Rs.10/- for children.
Place : Punganoor


The boat house located adjecent to the park with the boating for tourists is a pleasent expereince here at Yelagiri. Life jackets are given and the crew seems well trained. . A nice expereince worth the boat ride

FUNDERA PARK

Open Time : 09.30 AM - 6.30 PM
Entry fees : Rs 250 /-
Place : Nilovoor


Fundera Park is a place where your complete family can enjoy your holidays with pets in their natural habitat setup. We have more than 500 exotic birds and animals, none of them are caged and you can enter into their aviary and Interact with them. Also we have Petting farm, Farm animals, Ostriches, Emus. Cat House, Husky Play Area, lot of Exotic animals and Fishes

VELAVAN TEMPLE

Open Time : 06.00 AM - 8.00 PM

Place : Nilovoor


Velavan Temple is dedicated to worshipping Lord Murugan. Thetemple has a fantastic view of the surrounding hills and it houses a rare Gadothgajan, the son of Bheema, at its entrance.

SWAMI MALAI HILLS

'

Open Time : 06.00 AM - 8.00 PM
Entey Fee : Rs 350
Place :Mangalam


Yelagiri's most popular hill and one of the most breath taking as well, the Swami Malai Hill is the highest point of the hill station. A 3 km trek from the Punganoor lake will take you to the hill top.

SHIVAN TEMPLE

Open Time : 07.00 AM - 1.00 PM And 3.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place :Mangalam


Shiva temple is located at the foothills of Swamimalai, on Mangalam Road. Recently built temple has a Circular Garbha griha. Statues of Maharishis adorn the front yard. The Temple offers beautiful views of hills and valleys all around

PERUMAL TEMPLE

Open Time : 07.30 AM - 12.00 PM And 4.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place : Athanvoor


The temple has a few more shrines including Ramanujar, Manavala Mamunigal and Anjaneyar.It is said an ancient temple existed here centuries ago, and an ancient deity of Adi Kesava Perumal along with some lamps were excavated here while constructing the current temple.

NACHI AMMAN

Open Time : 07.00 AM - 1.00 PM And 3.00 PM - 7.00PM
Place : Nilavoor


Kadavu Nachi Amman temple, a devi temple situated on the banks of Nilavoor lake is a calm, serene place. One can walk to Nilavoor Cliff view point from here. The temple with a back drop of green hills around is quite scenic.

FOREST ENTERTAINMENT PARK

Open Time : 09.00 AM - 7.00 PM
Entey fee :Rs 250
Place : Mettukaniyur


The park is home to so many different birds, like parrots, pigeons, and chicks, and it's so colorful and vibrant. It truly gave me a sense of being in nature.

MOUNTAIN VIEW ADVENTURE PARK

Open Time : 09.00 AM - 7.00 PM
Entey fee : adult Rs 250 childrens RS 110
Place : kottur


Discover fun and adventure amidst nature and beauty. Visit us and experience a truly memorable adventure. We provide, 1) Zipline 2) Paintball 3) ATV 4) Bunjee Ejection 6) Giant Swing 6) Trekking to waterfall and many others

THRILL VALLEY

Open Time : 10.00 AM - 6.00 PM
Place : kottaiyour


The Thrill Valley is a lush green land filled with flora and fauna, bringing you closer to the beauty of nature. The intent was to create a ‘Resort’ of traditions and culture equipped with all the modern amenities over the mere idea of a conventional resort. Away from the city hub, The Thrill Valley is a perfect place to rejuvenate yourself with peace, adventure, and some fantastic food.

BIRDS PARK

Open Time : 9.00 AM - 5.30 PM
Enter fee : Rs 200
Place : Pudur


Yelagiri birds park is a great place to visit with family and friends. Birds There are a large variety of birds including several parrots, love birds, cockatoos and cockatiels, macaws, sun conures, red lory, ducks, pigeons, varities of chicken and more. Animals and reptiles There are goats, rabbits, snakes, turtles and chameleon

NILAVOOR CLIFF VIEW POINT

Open Time : Open 24 hours

Place : Nilavoor


The view was terrific and beautiful. It is the best place for sunrise view

In aqua fountain park having a Fountain . Birds There are a large variety of birds and it was inclue a small aquarium.

THANGA KOTTAI

Open Time : 10.00 AM - 9.30 PM
Entry fee :350 for 2 hr
Place : Kottaiyour


ARK Thanga Kottai aka ARK Golden Fort is a luxurious palace hotel with 105 Rooms. In every bit of the property, you can find lavish exteriors and luxury interiors. ARK Thangakottai comprises one of the largest swimming pools in the state with mammoth measurements of 128 ft. in length.
